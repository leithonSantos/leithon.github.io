The Growth of Gaming How the pandemic
has sparked fresh opportunities.

  • With lockdown measures and movement restrictions, many people have
    been spending more time at home,which has led to a significant increase
    in time spent playing video games. This has boosted sale of video games
    and consoles, as well as participation in online gaming platforms.

  • The need to maintain social connections during the pandemic has led to
    a surge in the popularity of online and multiplayer gaming. Games like
    "Among Us," "Fortnite," "Call of Duty: Warzone," and "Animal Crossing:
    New Horizons" have all seen spikes in popularity due to their ability
    to connect people across digital platforms.

  • The pandemic has attracted new players, including those who weren't
    previously video game fans. The accessibility and variety of games
    available have made more people interested in video games as a form
    of entertainment and socializing.
